      Bvsradio Website Change Brief
      Developer implementation instructions • bvsradio.com • 17 July 2026



          Objective: Improve navigation, audio-preview feedback, search-result clarity, the music-
          submission page structure, and the available service bundles.



      Requested changes

      1. Browse navigation
          Make the “Browse” control navigate or scroll the visitor to the Browse section.
          Ensure the destination is positioned correctly below any sticky header and works on desktop and
          mobile.

      Acceptance: Clicking “Browse” consistently lands on the Browse section without a broken link or
      incorrect offset.

      2. Audio-preview behavior and timing
          When the available preview snippet reaches its end, stop the player automatically.
          While a song is playing, show both the current playback time and the preview snippet’s total
          duration so listeners can immediately tell how much time is left.
          Use a familiar time display such as 0:12 / 0:30 (optionally paired with a progress bar or remaining-
          time label), and keep it synchronized while seeking or replaying.

      Acceptance: The preview never continues beyond the snippet, stops cleanly at its end, and displays
      accurate elapsed and total times throughout playback so the remaining preview time is clear.

      3. Search Results title
          Add a “Search Results” heading directly below the search bar and above the results list.
          Match the typography and visual style used by the site’s other section titles.
          Use appropriate spacing and alignment so the heading clearly belongs to the returned results.

      Acceptance: Search results have a clear, consistently styled title that remains well positioned at
      common screen sizes.

      4. Submit Music page cleanup
          Remove the redundant “Submit Music” header where the page already communicates that title.
          Reorganize the submission requirements into a clearer order.
          Group related requirements, place critical eligibility and file requirements first, and use short
          headings or bullets for easy scanning.
          Remove repeated information while preserving every unique requirement.

      Acceptance: Only one necessary page title remains, and an artist can quickly understand what is
      required and in what order to prepare it.

      5. Ultimate Bundle
          Add an “Ultimate Bundle” service option.
          The bundle must include mixing, mastering, and publishing.
          Present it consistently with the existing service/package cards, including a concise description,
          inclusions, price or enquiry action, and primary call to action as applicable.

      Acceptance: Visitors can clearly identify the Ultimate Bundle and understand that all three services
      are included.


      Implementation and QA guidance
          Reuse the site’s existing design system, spacing, typography, colors, and responsive breakpoints.
          Preserve keyboard navigation, visible focus states, semantic headings, and accessible labels.
          Test Browse navigation, search layout, and preview playback on desktop and mobile widths.
          Test audio completion, replay, pause/resume, seeking, and multiple previews to prevent
          overlapping playback.
          Confirm no existing submission requirements or package actions are lost during restructuring.


      Prepared from the consolidated Bvsradio review notes shared in Discord #general.
